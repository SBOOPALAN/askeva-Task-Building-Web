About Us

SPI Infra has been a stalwart in the construction industry, garnering a wealth of experience and expertise over the years. Founded in 2020, We have 20+ years of experience construction SPI Infra quickly rose to prominence under the leadership of Mr. B. Praveen Kumar, the Managing Partner. Mr. B.
